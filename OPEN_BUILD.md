# Open Build — GSF Ecosystem

GSF is currently in an open architecture phase.

This project is not positioned as a finished application,
but as a growing digital ecosystem seeking builders,
researchers, and system thinkers.

---

## Why Open Build?

GSF is designed as a long-term infrastructure project.

Instead of building silently,
development is opened early to allow:

- collaborative experimentation
- modular contributions
- independent system research
- architectural evolution
